# Loom Video Script - Flutter App Enhancement Sprint

## Version A - With Diagram
Goal:
Show how I will take your existing Flutter codebase over, plan work in Linear, and deliver tested progress with daily video catch-ups.
Time split:
0:00-0:15 Risk + approach
0:15-0:45 Day-1 audit + daily loop
0:45-1:15 Testing on physical device + Claude Max usage
1:15-1:30 Close + next step
Script:
Opening line:
“If this sprint gets unclear on scope, the app won’t feel finished in a month.”

Main talking points:
- Day 1: I’ll do a quick codebase audit, identify the highest-impact next fixes, and translate that into a short list of issues in `Linear`.
- Daily: we’ll do a short video catch-up, confirm what worked, update Linear, and lock the next smallest task for the following day.
- Implementation: I’ll use your Claude Max (200) access to speed up code changes and debugging, but I’ll keep decisions grounded in what your repo actually does.
- Testing: every day ends with verification on your real Android device in `Android Studio`—no emulator-only assumptions.

Closing CTA:
“Send me repo access and tell me your top 3 must-have outcomes. Then I’ll reply with a Week-1 Linear plan and our daily catch-up schedule.”
Diagram:
Repo audit -> Linear issue list -> Daily catch-up -> Build changes -> Test on device -> Linear update -> Next day
Step-by-step recording actions:
1. What to prepare before recording: open the job post and have notes ready for app state, test plan, and 1-month milestones.
2. What to show first on screen: show the repo folder structure (or a placeholder screenshot) and your Day-1 audit plan.
3. What to say in first 10-15 seconds: say the opening line about risk from unclear scope.
4. What to show/say in middle: show a mock `Linear` board (“Next 10 issues”) and point to the simple diagram chain.
5. How to close in final 10-15 seconds: show the device-testing checklist idea (short overlay is enough).
6. What CTA to end with: ask for repo access + top 3 must-have outcomes, then confirm you will post the Week-1 Linear plan.
