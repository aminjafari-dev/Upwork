# Zenzo Ads — Suggested milestones & pricing ($3,500 total)

**Job budget (fixed):** $3,500.00  
**Purpose:** Paste into Upwork **Submit a Proposal** → milestones. Amounts below are **client-facing totals** (before Upwork service fee). **You’ll receive** less; fee is calculated on the proposal page.

**Project duration (dropdown):** Choose **1 to 3 months** (fits an 8–12 week MVP slice).

## Option A — Four milestones (recommended)

Splits: **foundation → build → measure → ship**. Easier to defend in interviews than one giant payment.

| # | Amount | % of total | Suggested due date* |
|---|--------|------------|----------------------|
| **1** | **$875.00** | 25% | End of week **3** from contract start |
| **2** | **$1,050.00** | 30% | End of week **6** |
| **3** | **$1,050.00** | 30% | End of week **9** |
| **4** | **$525.00** | 15% | End of week **11–12** |

\*Pick **calendar dates** in Upwork when you apply (e.g. if you start Apr 1, M1 ≈ Apr 22).

**Total:** $875 + $1,050 + $1,050 + $525 = **$3,500.00**

### Copy-paste: milestone descriptions (Upwork fields)

**Milestone 1 — $875.00**  
**Title (short):** Foundation: auth, profiles, payment path  
**Description:** OTP + social login wired to your chosen auth provider; basic advertiser profile; payment method integration (test + production per your PSP); app environments and secrets hygiene; short doc on how to run builds. **Deliverable:** working flows on iOS + Android (staging), demo recording or call.

**Milestone 2 — $1,050.00**  
**Title (short):** Campaigns: creatives, schedule, links, targeting v1  
**Description:** Create/edit **text + image** ads; upload handling with validation; campaign **scheduling**; **external destination URLs**; **audience targeting** fields (location + community + interests per your MVP spec); campaign list and detail screens. **Deliverable:** end-to-end “draft → ready to run” on staging (delivery can be stubbed if backend is parallel).

**Milestone 3 — $1,050.00**  
**Title (short):** Delivery events + analytics dashboard  
**Description:** Impression/click (and minimum engagement) **event pipeline** aligned with backend; **performance dashboard**: views, clicks, CTR, spend visibility per campaign; basic charts/tables; empty/error states. **Deliverable:** demo with **real or staging** events; definitions of “view/click” documented.

**Milestone 4 — $525.00**  
**Title (short):** Hardening & launch readiness  
**Description:** Spend **caps/alerts** (as agreed); critical **bug fixes** from MVP testing; **store** checklist (icons, privacy strings, build flavors); handoff: README, env vars, known limitations, **phase 2** backlog. **Deliverable:** release candidate build(s) + short handoff doc.

## Notes for your proposal

- **Do not** leave milestone total at **$0** — Upwork requires amounts that sum to the agreed **$3,500**.  
- If the client wants **video ads** in MVP, either **increase budget** or **swap** scope (e.g. slimmer web or fewer targeting dimensions)—put that in writing before fixing milestone text.  
- **First milestone** should be **small enough** to feel fair, **large enough** to cover real setup (repos, auth, PSP accounts, builds).
